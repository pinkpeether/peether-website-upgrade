Cookie Policy

This policy explains how PTDT (Peether) uses cookies and similar technologies on our website and dApp.

Last Updated: January 2026

📖 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, understand how you use the site, and improve your overall experience.

Cookies can be "session cookies" (deleted when you close your browser) or "persistent cookies" (remain on your device for a set period).

🎯 How We Use Cookies

PTDT uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Operations: To ensure our website and dApp function correctly
  • Authentication: To keep you signed in and maintain your session
  • Preferences: To remember your settings and choices
  • Analytics: To understand how visitors use our platform
  • Security: To protect against fraud and unauthorized access

📂 Types of Cookies We Use

🔒

Necessary Cookies

Essential for the website to function. These cannot be disabled as they are required for core functionality like authentication, security, and session management.

📊

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting anonymous information. This data helps us improve our platform and user experience.

📢

Marketing Cookies

Used to track visitors across websites to display relevant advertisements. These cookies help us measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns.

⚙️

Functional Cookies

Enable enhanced functionality and personalization, such as remembering your language preference, display settings, and other customizations.

⚙️ Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have several options to manage cookies:

1. Through Our Cookie Banner

When you first visit our website, you'll see a cookie consent banner. You can:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Reject all optional cookies
  • Customize your preferences by category

2. Through Your Browser

Most browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • View what cookies are stored
  • Delete individual or all cookies
  • Block cookies from specific or all websites
  • Set preferences for first-party vs third-party cookies

3. Browser-Specific Instructions
